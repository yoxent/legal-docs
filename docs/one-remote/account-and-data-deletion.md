# Account and data deletion

**App:** One Remote (`com.vorithstudio.smarttvremote`)  
**Developer:** Vorith Studio (trade name of Vincent Oliver Veran, Philippines)  
**Contact:** vorithstudio@gmail.com  
**Last updated:** May 28, 2026

How to delete data associated with **One Remote**, as described in our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md).

---

## Summary

One Remote has **no user accounts**. Most data lives **on your device**. Voluntary **in-app feedback** may be stored in our feedback system (for example a spreadsheet behind a webhook).

---

## What you can delete

| Data | How to delete |
|------|----------------|
| **Saved TVs, layouts, pairing keys, settings** | Android **Settings → Apps → One Remote → Storage → Clear data**, or uninstall the App |
| **Pro entitlement** | Tied to Google Play / App Store — uninstalling does not cancel ownership; use store purchase history if needed |
| **Ad identifiers** | Android/iOS system settings → reset advertising ID / limit ad tracking |
| **In-app feedback you submitted** | Email **vorithstudio@gmail.com** (include approximate date and message excerpt) |
| **Support emails** | Email us and ask to delete your thread |
| **Store billing records** | Managed by Google or Apple — we cannot delete them |
| **Ad / measurement data (Google AdMob)** | Not stored on our servers — use device ad settings, in-app **Ad privacy settings** (when shown), or [Google ad settings](https://adssettings.google.com/) |

---

## Request a copy of your data (portability)

We do not host your saved TVs or layouts. We may hold **in-app feedback** and **support emails** you sent us.

Email **vorithstudio@gmail.com** with subject `Data access request — One Remote` and describe what you need (for example feedback you submitted). We respond within **30 days** where law requires.

---

## Request deletion by email

**vorithstudio@gmail.com**

**Subject:** `Data deletion request — One Remote`

Include what to delete (feedback, support emails, etc.). We respond within **30 days** where law requires.

## After deletion

- **On-device:** Cleared when you clear storage or uninstall.  
- **Feedback / email:** We delete or anonymize unless law requires retention.  
- **Ads:** Stop after uninstall; reset ad ID in system settings if desired.

## Pro, refunds, and subscriptions

**Pro** is a **one-time** non-consumable purchase (`one_remote_pro`), **not** a subscription. There is nothing to “cancel” except through a **refund** from the store that sold it.

| Topic | What to know |
|-------|----------------|
| **Who refunds** | **Google Play** (Android) or **Apple** (iOS) — we cannot refund or delete store payment records |
| **How to request** | [Google Play refunds](https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637?hl=en) · [Apple refunds](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204084) |
| **After refund** | Store removes **Pro**; the App goes back to the free tier (ads may return) |
| **Uninstall** | Does **not** refund **Pro**; entitlement stays on your store account until refunded or removed by the store |
| **Purchase help** | Email **vorithstudio@gmail.com** — we can guide you to the store; we cannot issue refunds |

Full terms: [Terms of Service — Refunds](terms-of-service.md#refunds) (Section 4).
