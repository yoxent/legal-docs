# Account and data deletion

**App:** One Remote (`com.vorithstudio.smarttvremote`)  
**Developer:** Vincent Oliver Veran  
**Contact:** vorithstudio@gmail.com  
**Last updated:** May 31, 2026

How to delete data associated with **One Remote**, as described in our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md).

---

## Summary

One Remote has **no user accounts**. Most data lives **on your device**. Voluntary **in-app feedback** may be stored in our feedback system (for example a spreadsheet behind a webhook).

---

## What you can delete

| Data | How to delete |
|------|----------------|
| **Saved TVs, layouts, pairing keys, settings** | Android **Settings → Apps → One Remote → Storage → Clear data**, or uninstall the App |
| **Pro entitlement** | Tied to Google Play / App Store — uninstalling does not cancel ownership; use store purchase history if needed |
| **Ad identifiers** | Android/iOS system settings → reset advertising ID / limit ad tracking |
| **In-app feedback you submitted** | Email **vorithstudio@gmail.com** (include approximate date and message excerpt) |
| **Support emails** | Email us and ask to delete your thread |
| **Store billing records** | Managed by Google or Apple — we cannot delete them |

---

## Request deletion by email

**vorithstudio@gmail.com**

**Subject:** `Data deletion request — One Remote`

Include what to delete (feedback, support emails, etc.). We respond within **30 days** where law requires.

---

## After deletion

- **On-device:** Cleared when you clear storage or uninstall.  
- **Feedback / email:** We delete or anonymize unless law requires retention.  
- **Ads:** Stop after uninstall; reset ad ID in system settings if desired.
